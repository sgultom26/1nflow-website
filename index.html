Vertical 01 · Live

1nFlow-LOS

A complete loan origination system covering UMKM, KPR, KKB, Multiguna, and KUR. Eight-stage BPMN-orchestrated workflow from RM intake to BI Fast disbursement, with audit trail on every decision.

  • ⚡ BPMN-orchestrated origination. Submission → KYC → Enrichment → Verification → Underwriting → Committee → Acceptance → Disbursement. Editable in BPMN designer; no Java change required to alter your workflow.
  • 🛡 Indonesian compliance built in. POJK 12/2017 (KYC), 18/2017 (SLIK), 23/2022 (BMPK), 1/2024 (DBR), 4/2022 (AI governance). Dukcapil eKYC + SLIK iDeb integrations live.
  • 🤖 AI credit narrative — advisory only. Anthropic Claude drafts the 5C analysis in 14 seconds; the credit officer reviews, edits, and signs. AI never makes binding decisions (POJK 4/2022).
  • 📊 Analytics built in. Pipeline health, time-to-decision distribution, NPL by product, officer productivity. No separate BI tool required.

Vertical 02 · Live

1nFlow-Appraisal

Manage every collateral valuation through one pipeline — KJPP eksternal + internal appraisers, mobile field capture with GPS-tagged photos, comparable analysis, senior review, signed reports. POJK 17/2020 + SPI 2018 compliant.

  • 📍 MAPPI directory lookup. Search 80+ Indonesian KJPPs by license tier (T, S, J), region, and specialty. Tier-based routing with capacity awareness.
  • 📱 Mobile field-capture app. iOS & Android. Offline-first. GPS-tagged photos, BPKB/SHM scan with OCR, voice notes. Syncs when back online.
  • 📐 Three valuation methods built in. Pendekatan Pasar (market comparison), Pendekatan Biaya (DRC), Pendekatan Pendapatan (income approach). SPI 2018 templates.
  • ⏰ POJK 40 revaluasi auto-tracker. 18-month and 30-month cycles auto-scheduled. Stop missing OJK deadlines because of an Excel reminder that didn't fire.

Vertical 03 · Q3 2026

1nFlow-Collections

An NPL workflow engine that orchestrates soft collection, hard collection, restructuring, and repossession through a single audit-logged pipeline. WhatsApp, SMS, voice, and field visit channels — coordinated rather than chaotic.

  • 💬 Multi-channel campaigns. WhatsApp Business API, SMS via Telkom / Indosat, voice IVR, RM field visits — all logged in one timeline per debtor. POJK 22/2024 collection-conduct compliance.
  • 🔄 Restructuring workflows. Reschedule, recondition, refinance — POJK 40/2019 compliant. Auto-recalculates DBR, generates Akta Perubahan, updates SLIK.
  • 📍 Field collection app. Mobile app for field officers. Visit logs, payment promises, receipts, GPS verification. Syncs to back-office in real time.
  • ⚖️ Recovery & repossession tracking. Lelang KPKNL workflows, lawyer assignment, recovery rate analytics, write-off accounting integration.

1nFlow-Treasury

Treasury & ALM for tier-2 banks and BPRs that need real-time visibility into liquidity, GAP, and BMPK without buying a tier-1 treasury system. Integrated directly with your core banking and 1nFlow-LOS pipeline.
